We Are Karn

Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals face significant challenges in communicating with hearing people, especially in public spaces such as hospitals, airports, government offices, schools, and retail stores. This communication barrier stems from the fact that most hearing individuals are not fluent in sign language, and deaf individuals often rely on lip-reading or written communication, which can be inefficient and prone to misunderstandings. The lack of accessible communication tools in public places exacerbates the problem, leading to frustration, exclusion, and even discrimination for deaf individuals.

Karn being a Sign Language Translator provides several services to deafs which include smooth communication,and data security!
We are the ones who resolve the above problems by acting as a medium of communication between the deaf and the hearing. We provide a platform where deaf individuals can communicate with hearing individuals through a sign language interpreter, who translates spoken language into sign language and vice versa. Our interpreters are trained professionals who are fluent in both sign language and spoken language, ensuring accurate and effective communication between the two parties. By bridging the communication gap between the deaf and the hearing, we empower deaf individuals to access essential services, participate in social activities, and engage with the world around them.
